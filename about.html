GO STUDIO DESIGN

I'm Giulia Obert, a designer always looking for new inspirations. I am an interior designer, a communicator and a ux-ui specialist. I'm a three-dimensional thinker.
Creativity and planning coexist in me. An alchemy necessary to complete a project, be it an interior design, a communication, or an ux-ui one. I am convinced that once learned the designer approach it is applicable to different areas of design. My career starts with a desire to design interior spaces, make them unique and planned on the single person needs. Then I wanted to tell the stories behind it, sharing the process and the final results online. More recently the desire to deepen the design of the interaction that takes place between human beings and computer systems.
